I am a Stand Builder

How does Yours Stand Builder work?

Exhibitor companies post a Project detailing what stand build they require, where they need it and when. They can upload a design idea/example and other relevant additional information.

Stand builders can then access the details of the Project through their own online account, and review the exact requirements to see if they would be able to provide the requested stand build service.

Each Project has an open Q&A board where stand builders can ask clarification questions to the exhibitor, to explain the finer details.

Stand builders can them submit their price quote for the Project, privately through YSB, and await the exhibitor's decision. If their bid is accepted - they get the job. It's that simple!
